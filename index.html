    Why Choose Us?

    GITC is not a business to make a profit from trainings, GITC foundation is supported by the Fund for Armenian relief and American-Armenian donors to co-finance students tuitions and promote technological capacity building, especially among the people interested in programming. GITC has an innovative approach and market-driven curriculum offering its courses also to vulnerable groups free of charge. Our trainers are representing the biggest technological companies in Armenia with more than 7 years of experience. With the aid of our talented instructors and cutting-edge teaching techniques, students not only learn or improve their technical skills but are also introduced to the working culture and industry environment with the possibility of employment in the top IT companies. The programs provide comprehensive, ongoing assessments to determine the students learning style, strengths, areas of needed instruction, and progress toward goals.

    What is the format of the course and at what time?

    The courses are online for everyone regardless of the location and offline courses are held at GITC offices in Gyumri and Yerevan. We suggest daytime and evening time schedules

    How many times per week and how long per day is the course duration?

    Each course duration per week is 6 hours scheduled 2 days per week with 3 hours

    Does GITC give a certificate after graduation?

    Upon completion of the training we give certificates to our students. Moreover, those whose performance is above 80% receive certificates of excellence
