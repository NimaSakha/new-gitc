    Ensuring employment through capacity building

    • Date November 9, 2022
    • Comments 0 comment

    Gyumri Information Tecnologies Center has started another important cooperation with HSBC Armenia.

    HSBC Armenia, emphasizing the balanced development and empowerment of regions by promoting job creation through technological education, finances and supports the implementation of “Ensuring employment through capacity building” project in Tavush region. Socially disadvantaged individuals living in Tavush region, sufferers from Artshakh war and single mothers can apply for this program.

    Within the framework of the program, the relevant beneficiaries have the opportunity to participate in the following courses free of charge:

    • Computer skills/basic skills
    • Web Programming (HTML/CSS/JavaScript)
    • Social Media Marketing (SMM)
    • Graphic Design

    The courses have been held in a hybrid online and classroom format in cooperation with private companies in the field.

    In addition to technical knowledge, the participants have received soft skills, as well as participated in interesting social events in Tavush region with HSBC Armenia and GITC representatives.
